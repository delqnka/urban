Delyana · Founder and Colorist
01Story

A salon devoted to one craft.

For over a decade, Delyana has worked exclusively with color. Studying how light behaves on the hair, how shade reads on the skin, how a quiet tonal shift can change the way a woman carries herself.

She trained between Sofia, Milan and London. Returned to Varna with a single intention. To build a small, devoted studio where color is treated as a discipline, and the appointment is treated as a ritual.

Each booking begins with a long, unhurried conversation. The color comes after.

"Color shouldn't announce itself. It should make you feel more like yourself."
02Practice

Quietly remarkable, by design.

The studio takes a small number of appointments each week. Every transformation is handled personally, from the consultation through the final styling. This is the only way the work stays at the standard her clients return for.
