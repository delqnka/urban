05The Studio

A room for light, and time.

Three chairs. Windows facing north. Espresso and silence on request. The studio is designed around one idea. That a great color appointment is, first, a great morning.

01Atmosphere

Slow light. Soft surfaces. Considered silence.

The room is built to be quiet. Linen drapes, lime washed walls, a long pale oak counter. Light is allowed to do the work. The mirrors are positioned to read color accurately in every season.

You are offered an espresso, a tea, or nothing at all. Some clients work. Some sleep. Some read. The studio adapts to the woman in the chair.
